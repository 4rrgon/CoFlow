About CoFlow

Connecting learners through collaborative study groups

Our Mission

CoFlow is a dynamic platform designed to connect students and learners in collaborative study groups tailored to their preferences and schedules. We foster a productive learning community through personalized profiles, direct messaging, calendar integration, and a constructive rating system.

Core Features

User Profiles

Personalized profiles including academic interests, bio, and customizable calendar views to coordinate study sessions.

Group Management

Intuitive tools to create, join, and manage study groups with customizable parameters for size, duration, and scheduling.

Progressive Web App

Available as a standalone mobile application on both iOS and Android devices for seamless studying anywhere.

Achievement System

Custom badges reward consistent participation and helpfulness, with optional public display on profiles.
